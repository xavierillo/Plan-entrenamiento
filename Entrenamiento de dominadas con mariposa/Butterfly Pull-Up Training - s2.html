Notes for Week 2, Day 1

After warmup:

Complete all sets of Strict BUP Straight Body Circles
*they do not have to be unbroken but if you can do unbroken with GREAT form, that is the goal
-rest approx 45 sec per round -

THEN

Setup the Target Drill first...then...
On a running 3min clock:
Work on the target drill.
-If you feel rushed with the running clock get about 3-5min of practice with this drill.
The above is NOT for "reps" but for quality! So the time is just a suggestion.

This program is an advanced program!

Prerequisite strength:
6-10 slow and controlled pull-ups
Healthy shoulders!
Excellent command of Kipping CTB Pullups

We take you through Prehab warmups to keep shoulders mobile and strong. Then, you will approach each day with a variety of strict strength drills, muscle memory movements and eventually kipping butterfly pullups.

THIS SKILL IS NOT RECOMMENDED FOR MOST ATHLETES.
If you have any pain while kipping or cannot fulfill the prerequisite strength requirements we recommend you continue with our other pulling programs to build more strength.
