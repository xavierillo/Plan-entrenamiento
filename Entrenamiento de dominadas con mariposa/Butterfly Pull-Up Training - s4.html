Notes for Week 4, Day 1

Warmup:
Start with 2min of Lat Eccentric
Then complete 15 Behind the Neck Pullups

DRILL IT:
Today we start the last week of the program & want to start building a more consistent rhythm.
Therefore, you are to accumulate 30 GREAT target drill reps.

*When you feel "wonky" or out of control, drop and start a new set
Try to really FEEL the TIMING of hitting the object & then immediately driving toes back to start.
REMEMBER LONG FLUID SCOOP from arch to hollow!

Notes for Week 4, Day 4

For WarmUp:
3 rounds of:
Face Pull Y's
Hanging Scap Circles
Arched PullUps

THEN:
5 sets [as many reps as you can control safely]....INCREASE circle "size" each time.
*start with mini circles like you have practiced and each rep wait a bit longer to hit your imaginary target (the higher the target the bigger the BUP}.

By set 3 or 4 you should be at a regular size BUP like the one in video.

CUES TO REMEMBER:
1] Long scoop while keeping shoulder muscles active
2} Elbows pull back & out slightly
3} Try to pull the bar towards you as you scoop
4] LEAN BACK SLIGHTLY TO ANGLE YOUR CHEST SO YOU DO NOT HIT YOUR CHIN!
[look at the angle of my chest on video]

CONGRATS ON FINISHING YOUR TRAINING...
This is just a start & you can run thru this program again if you need more practice.

If you loved this program and want to see what else we have make sure you head to our website:
---- TheBarbellPhysio.com ----
