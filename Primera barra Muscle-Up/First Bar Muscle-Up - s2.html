Notes for Week 2, Day 1

After completing your mobility:

3 rounds alternating:
5 reps BMU Pull Downs
15 Scoop to Hollow Drill
*for quality not time!

-THEN-

Do 4 sets of BMU 3 Position Drill
one set is:
1 Lat pulldown hold
1 Lat hollow jump
1 Pull around into support
[rest approx 15-20 sec between sets]
**If you are afraid to do this on high bar - then it can be done on a low bar as well

ALWAYS SECURE BAR TO RIG VERY WELL when doing any drill with barbell on rig.
*Never ever do any drill without securing AND checking that equipment is safe before doing drill.

Notes for Week 2, Day 2

After mobility complete:

3 round of:
15 arch/hollow tension drill
5 banded 3 position BMU drill
4/3/2 strict CTB pullups *weight them for more challenge
[descending reps for CTB each round]

The 3 Rounds ARE NOT for time. Do them for quality.
* If you do bad reps you will have a hard time getting a muscle-up.

ALWAYS SECURE BAR TO RIG VERY WELL when doing any drill with barbell on rig.
*Never ever do any drill without securing AND checking that equipment is safe before doing drill.

Notes for Week 2, Day 4

After mobility:

Complete ALL sets of the BMU Pull-Down Drill THEN move to Banded 3 position Drill

*For Pull-Down Drill
*Hold at top for 5sec EACH rep!
*rest 20-30sec between each set but if you need more to be successful at all reps that is ok!

THEN:

For 3 Position Banded BMU
2 x 3 reps
2 x 2 reps
1 x 1 rep
[approx 30 sec rest but if you need more between sets that is fine]
