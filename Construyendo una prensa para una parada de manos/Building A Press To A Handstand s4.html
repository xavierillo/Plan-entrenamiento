Notes for Week 4, Day 1

Complete each skill for all rounds before moving to the next skill

5sets x 10reps Hollow Body Pull Downs
-20sec rest between sets-
Choose a band that is challenging but do-able to complete all 10 reps unbroken

5-3-1 Three-Position Press Lower
You will do 5 reps of each position on round 1 then 3 reps then 1rep per position.
-rest approx 45 sec between sets-

4sets x 10rep V-Up
-rest 20 sec between sets-
It is okay if you have to break up the reps during the sets. They do not have to be unbroken.

*Rest time is recommendation. If you need more, no problem!

Notes for Week 4, Day 4

Complete one set of:

-15 reps of Low Ring "TTB"
[if you don't have access to a low bar do 30 Banded Hollow Pull Downs]
-20 reps Pike Drag Drill
-10 Press to Handstand Elevated

TIME TO TRY OUT A PRESS HANDSTAND?!
Think you are close? Give it a go...watch my video for some extra tips and visualize your body doing what you see in the video.

CONGRATS ON FINISHING YOUR PROGRAM!
Did you attempt a press and find success? Make sure you DM or email us and share the good news.
Whether it is the full skill or just better than where you began 4 weeks ago....we want to know!

Not quite ready to attempt the skill or not where you want to be?

No problem! Remember, these skills take months or even years to master, but the more you work on drills and strength, the closer you will be to your goal.
We recommend either repeating the program or joining our monthly membership where we can work strength, mobility and form with a variety of new drills [see below].
