Notes for Week 3, Day 1

Warmup:
3 x 5 reps [per arm] with a 3 sec hold Rack Lift Offs
--rest about 20-30 sec between each set--

Then:
Alternate between Hollow Body Wall Walks & HSPU Tempo Drill [on box with a tempo lower]

for example round 1 you will do:
2 Hollow Body Wall Walks
3 Handstand Lowers with a 3 sec. lower & regular count on way up
**each round the wall walks increase & lowers decrease

[tension is key - squeeze everything]
