This 8 week program is designed to be performed one day per week to help functional fitness athletes improve their snatch technique and strength.

Built for athletes of two levels:
1. Athletes newer to Olympic lifting wanting more detailed help understanding technique
2. Intermediate level Olympic lifters who want 1 day per week of specialization on their Olympic lifting

Weights and loading used throughout the program will vary based on your goal and experience level.
