2.2
  • Print daily summary

7 min EMOM [every min on the minute]
7 V-Up pass thru + 9 donkey kick with "burpee"

Within ONE minute you will complete all the work above.
You will do that for 7 minutes (basically 7 rounds)
** if you finish in 35 seconds then you have 25sec to rest before the next round starts)

NOTE: You should have atleast 15 sec of rest each round so if you don't then decrease reps on each skill.

Don't forget your Cobra cool-down stretch.
