Notes for Week 1, Day 1

Warmup: 1 min of wrists

Skill Work:

2 x 20reps of Handstand Shoulder Shrugs
-rest 40sec between sets-

Then:

Accumulate 1 min OF handstand shaping by:
starting in pike & walk out to a HOLLOW position by:
1) extanding shoulders to an open position
2) arms covering ears
3) pressing thru ground
4) pulling ribs down and tucking pelvis under "tuck your tail"
THEN:
return back to pike position & repeat

Rest time is just a recommendation.
*We would rather see completion for quality as this is not for speed.

Notes for Week 1, Day 2

Warmup:
1min of Wrist Warmup
45 sec of Overhead Opener [keep arms shoulder width]

Skill Work:
Complete 4 rounds of Handstand Holds off Box:
30sec-20sec-15sec-10sec
[each goes to decreasing sec of holds]
-rest approx 30 sec between sets-

Then:

Complete 2 sets x 20reps of Handstand Shift & Lift:
[10 per arm - you can double reps if you need more of a challenge]
- rest enough to be successful at all reps -

HELPFUL HINTS:
[higher the box, easier on hamstrings or bend knees if you have tight hamstrings & not able to get shoulders stacked over wrists bc of lack of mobility]

Rest time is just a recommendation.
*We would rather see completion for quality as this is not for speed.
