Notes for Week 1, Day 1

3x20 Spanish Squats - if the first set isn't 7/10 difficult then grab a 35/20lb weight for sets 2 and 3

Then 3 rounds of:
- 30 seconds lateral taps per side
- 30 seconds extreme quad stretch
- 30 seconds rest
(Each round takes 2 minutes to complete)
